
# ABABIL Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the ABABIL project a welcoming, respectful, and professional experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful to all community members.
- Accepting constructive feedback.
- Collaborating professionally.
- Helping new contributors.
- Focusing on improving ABABIL.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Hate speech.
- Personal attacks.
- Spam or malicious activities.
- Sharing false or misleading information.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate these standards.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all official ABABIL community spaces.
